Historical Sports News

1999 FIFA Women's World Cup Final

The 1999 FIFA Women's World Cup final was held at the Rose Bowl in Pasadena, California. The game ended in a dramatic penalty shootout, with Brandi Chastain scoring the winning goal and celebrating with her iconic pose.

Muhammad Ali vs. Sonny Liston (1965)

The rematch between Muhammad Ali and Sonny Liston in 1965 is one of the most famous boxing matches in history. Ali knocked out Liston in the first round with a controversial punch, known as the "phantom punch."

Jesse Owens at the 1936 Berlin Olympics

Jesse Owens' performance at the 1936 Berlin Olympics is one of the most significant moments in sports history. Owens won four gold medals, challenging Hitler's propaganda and becoming a symbol of resistance against racism.

Diego Maradona's 'Hand of God' Goal (1986)

Maradona scored the infamous 'Hand of God' goal during the 1986 FIFA World Cup quarter-final match between Argentina and England, using his hand to punch the ball into the net.

Michael Jordan's 'The Shot' (1989)

Michael Jordan's iconic game-winning shot during the 1989 NBA Playoffs against Craig Ehlo of the Cleveland Cavaliers solidified Jordan's reputation as a clutch performer.
